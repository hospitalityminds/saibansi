About Us


Located in Navi Mumbai, Hotel Sai Bansi Residency is 8 km from Sagar Vihar and offers various facilities, such as a garden. The property is set 8 km from Inorbit Mall, Vashi and 11 km from DY Patil Cricket Stadium. Utsav Chowk is 12 km from the hotel and Kharghar Hills is 12 km away. All guest rooms in the hotel are fitted with a flat-screen TV. Each room comes with a private bathroom. At Hotel Sai Bansi Residency, rooms come with a seating area.
